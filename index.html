What is a QR Code and how does it work?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of barcode that can be scanned with a smartphone camera, instantly directing users to digital content like websites, payment systems, or contact information.

Do I need a special app to scan QR Codes?

No, most modern smartphones have built-in camera apps that can scan QR codes directly. Simply open your camera, point it at the code, and follow the on-screen prompt.

What’s the difference between static and dynamic QR Codes?

A static QR code has fixed information that cannot be changed after it's generated. A dynamic QR code allows you to edit the linked content anytime, track scan data, and update details without changing the code.

Are QR Codes secure to use?

Yes, QR codes themselves are safe, but always ensure that the destination or content they link to is trusted. Scanning a QR code from a reliable source ensures a secure experience.

What kind of information can a QR Code store?

QR codes can link to websites, social media profiles, payment systems, contact details, videos, PDFs, and much more. Dynamic QR codes also allow for real-time updates to the content.

Can I customize the design of my QR Code?

Yes! You can create custom QR codes with your brand’s colors, logo, and design elements to make them more attractive and aligned with your business image.
